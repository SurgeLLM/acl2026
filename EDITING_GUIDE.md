# Editing Guide (Organizers)

This website is meant for workshop audiences (authors + attendees). Keep pages lightweight, scannable, and action-oriented.

## Where to edit content

For ACL 2026, the primary content lives in:

- Workshop metadata + CTAs: `_data/acl2026/site.yml`
- Navigation: `_data/acl2026/navigation.yml`
- Important dates: `_data/acl2026/dates.yml`
- Submission links/policies: `_data/acl2026/submission.yml`
- Topics: `_data/acl2026/topics.yml`
- Invited speakers: `_data/acl2026/speakers.yml`
- Organizers + roles: `_data/acl2026/organizers.yml`
- Program committee: `_data/acl2026/committee.yml`
- Draft schedule: `_data/acl2026/program.yml`
- FAQ: `_data/acl2026/faq.yml`
- Updates: `_data/acl2026/updates.yml`

## What to optimize for

1) **Submissions**: make the “why submit”, deadlines, and “how to submit” obvious on the home page.
2) **Clarity**: use short sections, bullets, and FAQs.
3) **Trust**: keep speaker statuses clear (confirmed vs pending).
4) **Findability**: keep nav labels familiar (CFP, Program, Speakers, Dates) and use the search modal.
